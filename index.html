About STAVYA 2k22

Stavya is the flagship cultural fest of Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara International Campus Diu. This is the three-days long fest which will inherit whole spectrum of cultural diverseness. We have five major events supported and administered under their respective clubs. Adding to that, we also have three quiz events.
