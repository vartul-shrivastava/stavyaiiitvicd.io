ABOUT F.R.E.I.N.D.S

Friends is an American television sitcom created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, which aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004, lasting ten seasons. With an ensemble cast starring Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc, Matthew Perry and David Schwimmer, the show revolves around six friends in their 20s and 30s who live in Manhattan, New York City.

F.R.I.E.N.D.S QUIZ FORMAT

  • The quiz will consist of three rounds.
  • The questions will revolve around the show, its characters, events, etc.
  • First-round will be conducted through a Google form-based quiz which will consist of around 25 questions and should be submitted within the given time limit.
  • The second round will be conducted as a live quiz on the Kahoot App along with a Google Meet.
  • The third round will be conducted on Google Meet.

F.R.I.E.N.D.S QUIZ RULES

  • The participant has to be enrolled as a student in any Indian College/Institute/ University.
  • Participants have to register for the quiz beforehand, in teams of two.
  • The team can discuss among themselves separately, and answers should be given by only one of them.
  • The entire duration of the Google meeting will be recorded in every round, if any vulgarity is found strict action will be taken against the team.
  • In case of any dispute, the final decision will be taken by the organizers.
