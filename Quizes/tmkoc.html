ABOUT TMKOC

Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah (TMKOC) is a Hindi sitcom based on the weekly column "Duniya Ne Undha Chasma" by Taarak Mehta in Chitralekha magazine. It is produced by Asit Kumar Modi. It premiered on 28 July 2008 and airs on Sony Sab and is also digitally available on SonyLIV.

TMKOC QUIZ FORMAT

  • The quiz will consist of three rounds.
  • The questions will revolve around the show, its characters, events, etc.
  • First-round will be conducted through a Google form-based quiz which will consist of around 25 questions and should be submitted within the given time limit.
  • The second round will be conducted as a live quiz on the Kahoot App along with a Google Meet.
  • The third round will be conducted on Google Meet.

TMKOC QUIZ RULES

  • The participant must be enrolled as a student in any Indian Institute/College/University.
  • For the first round, instructions will be given on Google Meet and a quiz will be conducted through a Google Form.
  • For the second round, instructions and questions will be given on Google Meet and the quiz will be conducted on Kahoot App.
  • For the Third round, questions will be asked on Google Meet itself from each team.
  • The whole meet will be recorded, if any vulgarity is found strict action will be taken against the individual.
  • In case of any dispute, the final decision will be taken by the organizers.
