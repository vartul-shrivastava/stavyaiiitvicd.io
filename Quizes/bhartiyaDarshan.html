Bhartiya darshan QUIZ FORMAT

  • This 3-Rounds event will be a deep dive into Indian culture and history covering topics from all aspects like art, music, dance, religion, food, heritage, different cultures, monuments, India’s history from ancient times to modern world.
  • The participants can be a part of this event either solo or maximum in a team of two.
  • Round 1: The first round will be conducted on the Kahoot app. It will have around 30 MCQ questions based on everything from India’s diverse culture. Top 20 teams or a total of 40 participants will qualify for the second round.
  • Round 2: The second round will be conducted on google meet. There will be 20-25 questions about historical places, monuments and the history of india. Top 10 teams will be moving on to the next and final round.
  • Round 3: The last round will be an interactive Q&A quiz on google meet. There will be 4 questions per team + a rapid fire round. The topics covered will be famous heroes and personalities of India from ancient times to the modern world.

Bhartiya darshan QUIZ RULES

  • The participants can be any student pursuing his/her studies from any universities/colleges across the country.
  • The participants will be able to register for the event through the Google Forms circulated.
  • If a participant’s action raises suspicion of the organizers, their candidature can be questioned.
  • For the Third round, questions will be asked on Google Meet itself from each team.
  • The organizers can take such decisions to ensure a fair competitive environment.
  • In case of any discrepancies, the decision of the organizers will be final and binding.
