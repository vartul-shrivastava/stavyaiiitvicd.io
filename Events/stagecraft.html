STAGECRAFT FORMAT

  • The participant must be enrolled as a student in any Indian College/University/Institute.
  • Participants can submit only one entry per topic. Participants are ALLOWED to submit their entries on each topic.
  • The Video entry should be at least 3 minutes long and at most 5 minutes long.
  • The language of the dialogues can be Hindi, English or a combination of Both.
  • The Video format should be MP4 or MKV.
  • The Video entries should be collected through a google form shared with all the participants.
