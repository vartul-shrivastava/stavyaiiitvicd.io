ABOUT Malhar

Malhar is the flagship music event which continues its legacy from Summer Break Events 2k21. It is a singing competiton which aims to attract best of vocals from around the country, and aims to provide a platform to showcase their singing skills and beyond. This is a theme-based competition where the participant is needed to choose a song in context with the theme. For more details, download the brochure given below.
Win Prizes Worth ₹3.5K!

MALHAR THEMES

  • Bollywood 00’s (Any bollywood song from 2000-2009)
  • Bhakti Geet (Any devotional song which is in hindi language)

MALHAR RULES

  • The participant must be enrolled as a student in any Indian Institute/College/University.
  • Participants can submit only one entry per theme.
  • The duration of the song must be at least 1 minute and at most 4 minutes.
  • Participants must submit a video in which they are singing, along with an audio file. Video must be in MP4 format and audio in MP3.
  • If a participant is using background music like karaoke or an instrument, it must be attached separately as an audio file in MP3 format.
  • If the music is taken from some source, it must be mentioned accordingly. Failing to do so will result in the entry becoming null and void.
  • Plagiarism won't be tolerated. If the participant is found to be involved in such practice, then his participation will be disqualified.
